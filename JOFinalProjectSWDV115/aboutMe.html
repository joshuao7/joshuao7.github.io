A little about me...

I grew up here in Boise but have lived in Montana and Colorado. Out of high school I joined the nursing program here at CWI but dropped out of it due to not enjoying the content or the field. I took a gap year which turned into about 5 gap years. During that time I worked in industries like, Construction, Sales, Real Estate, Caregiving, and Hospitality. In my personal time I dabbled in web design and made a few static websites for friends and family. This last summer I was living in Moscow, Idaho and decided to come back to Boise to pursue this degree in software-development. I have enjoyed it more than I expected and plan to continue at BSU when I finish. In my free time you can find me in the mountains, playing pickle-ball, or reading a book.
